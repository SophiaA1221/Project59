Reciting Poems/Speeches

About

__________________

Reciting poems is not about what you recite, but how you recite it. It’s no exception for me. I have memorized and recited many poems and speeches. That’s my favorite hobby. I like doing these things because you almost feel what the poet or writer is feeling. If the speech is sad, you feel sad. If the speech is empty, then you feel empty, et cetera.

Favorites

This is my favorite poem:

The Road Not Taken

By Robert Frost

Two roads diverged in a yellow wood,
And sorry I could not travel both
And be one traveler, long I stood
And looked down one as far as I could
To where it bent in the undergrowth;

Then took the other, as just as fair,
And having perhaps the better claim
Because it was grassy and wanted wear,
Though as for that the passing there
Had worn them really about the same,

And both that morning equally lay
In leaves no step had trodden black.
Oh, I kept the first for another day!
Yet knowing how way leads on to way
I doubted if I should ever come back.

I shall be telling this with a sigh
Somewhere ages and ages hence:
Two roads diverged in a wood, and I,
I took the one less traveled by,
And that has made all the difference.

My Works

Here are some of the poems that I have written:

Work (the POEM, not the category)

I’m busy as a bumblebee,
I have to do my chores.
I have to wash the dishes
And I have to sweep the floors!
It’s not so fun to do this stuff;
Your hands will hurt too bad.
But work is nothing you should play,
It sure will make you sweat all day,
So work real fast without delay!
Or I will be so sad.

I hate work, for it’s so boring!
I really do admit.
The toiling, coiling, everything!
I’m still mosquito-bit.
But I don’t mind, my work is fun,
I do still have to say,
' I’ve worked so hard, but now it’s done!
I really have pleased everyone.
I’m done with work. I have to run!'
Now it’s time to play.




23 Times; A Reference to Caesar

23 times, 23 times,
They stabbed Caesar 23 times.
Violent to the eye,
They stabbed Caesar 23 times.
Print or cursive, however you write,
They stabbed Caesar 23 times.
Senate wasn’t that surprised,
They stabbed Caesar 23 times.
Oracle said, ‘Watch out for the Ides!’
They stabbed Caesar 23 times.
